okay, there's enough crap in here that I should actually document it.

Arrow keys - Move the camera
A and shift A - Move the camera on the Z axis. (in and out of the screen)
Drag the mouse to rotate the model around.
ESC - Quit
s - toggle cell shading
o - toggle outline rendering
v - toggle vertex array rendering
[ - lower LOD level by 0.05
] - raise LOD level by 0.05
